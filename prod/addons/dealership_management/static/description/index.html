Business Expansion on another level

A business is built with ideas and hardwork; however, to expand your business you need to think of an extraordinary way

Making your products or services available worldwide is an option for business expansion. Yes of course, opening a showroom or service centre all around the world can cost you a lot.

Here, comes the concept of Dealership!

Dealership is a method in which you authorize others to sell your products or services in a particular area or region. Basically, the product is yours, the retailer will buy your products and then sell it out on the market with the brand name of yours.

Moreover, you can also add your terms and conditions in the contract of the dealership to avoid any type of conflict further.

Dealership Management in Odoo

You need excellent software to expand your business exceptionally worldwide!

Odoo is a complete business management software; so, why not manage dealerships in Odoo.

Odoo Dealership Management facilitates you to manage dealerships and the related contracts from start to end of the process. It is used to manage each step perfectly so that you don't need to face any type of hassles.

The module has everything from registering dealers to an e-commerce for your customers who can find dealers in their region or the nearest location.

Hence, there is nothing wrong in saying that dealership management is simplified in Odoo.

Approve or Reject Dealers as you want

As an admin, you have authority to approve or reject any dealership. Moreover, you can take the decision on the basis of the history of the business of the dealer.

It helps you to analyze the dealer whether he/she is worth it to handle the dealership without hassles.

You can dealers can go for service based or showroom based dealership as per his/her budget.

Customers can Locate the Dealers From Worldwide

The module Odoo Dealership Management allows your customers to locate the dealers worldwide via map and data cards.

The customers can check the review of the dealers and can select the dealers on the basis of ratings. Moreover, the customers can also give review to the dealer as per the service.

Not only this, the customers can mark their current location to the location of the dealers.

The customer can directly contact the dealer or to you for the requirement of purchase; if the customer contacts you then you can assign the lead to any dealer you find suitable.

Newly Designed Dashboard for Easy Tracking

Admin Dashboard

  • Card View to analyze monthly stats like Dealer Registration, products, leads and sale.
  • Progress bar to track the lead.
  • Donut chart and line graph to analyze registration on the basis of their state.
  • Bar Graph to track the trending products as well as dealers.
  • Donut chart to track dealers on the basis of filters like plan wise and contract state wise.
  • Card view to check total number of registrations and the number of dealers.
  • Linear and donut chart to track the hike in sale.
  • Map view to locate the dealers from worldwide.

Dealer Dashboard

  • Dealer can check his/her personal information from the profile section.
  • In my product section, the dealer can check his own products.
  • The dealer can check all the products available from all products section.
  • Dealers can track all his/her orders.
  • From lead section, he/she can check all the assigned leads.
  • Dealer can also check the validity of his/her contract with the help of the donut chart.
