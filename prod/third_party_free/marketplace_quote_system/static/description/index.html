Odoo Marketplace Quote System

The module adds the sales quote feature to the product pages of Odoo marketplace. Now, the customers interested in bulk purchase can send a sales quote for any product to the admin enclosing the product quantity he wants to purchase and price at which he wishes to purchase it. The admin can then accept/reject the quote as required in Odoo.

Adds A Sales Quote Process On Odoo Marketplace Website

The module facilitates you to operate B2C and B2B marketplace at the same place.

Automates you sales quotation process for admin, sellers and customers on Odoo marketplace

  • You can sell the products to regular customers and also accept sales quotes from wholesale buyers looking for bulk purchase.
  • The 'Send Quote' option can be added to any desired product(s) on the Odoo Marketplace webpage.
  • Every request can be tracked and responded using Odoo. The admin and seller can accept/reject the quote and the customer can track it status on his website account.
  • Provide flexibility for interested wholesale buyers to make a bulk buy request for the products directly from the Marketplace website.

Detailed Features List

Adds a sales quotation & proposal system in Odoo Marketplace

    The module lets you accept bulk sales quotes from interested buyers on Odoo marketplace. The whole buyers can now submit their quotes for the allowed products directly from the website. The admin and seller can approve/reject the submitted quotes from the Odoo backend.

Facilitates bulk product purchase by customers at a proposed price

    Your regular customers can add the product to cart and buy it as usual. In addition, now interested wholesale buyers can submit the sale quote for the bulk purchase of a product. The submitted quote shows the desired quantity of the product and the proposed price per quantity that buyer wishes to purchase.

Choose which products are allowed to accept sales quote

  • The admin and seller can choose which product to allow for Sales quote on Odoo Marketplace.
  • The allowed product shows the sales quote ribbon on the shop page to attract wholesale buyers.
  • Now, the product page of these products also shows 'Send Quote' button.
  • These products can also be purchased as usual at the regular price.

Manage received Sales quotations from Odoo backend

  • The submitted quote can be managed from the backend.
  • The admin and seller can approve/reject the sales quote as required.
  • The status of the quote changes from pending to approved or rejected, in-cart, etc.

Allows the buyer to track his sales quote status from website account

  • The buyer can track the status of his quote from his website account.
  • The status change is reflected in close to real-time to the buyer.
  • The status appears as pending, approved, or rejected.

Allows approved quotes to be directly added to cart

  • The buyer can add his approved sales quote to the cart directly from his website account.
  • The proposed quantity and approved price automatically appear in the cart.
  • The buyer cannot change the quantity of the sales quote in the cart.
  • Once the buyer adds an approved quote to his cart, the status changes to ‘in-cart’ in the backend.

Allows two-way communication between admin/seller & Buyer

  • The admin / seller and the buyer can communicate using Odoo chatter.
  • The chatter appears in the quote form in the customer's website account.
  • Every communication is saved in Odoo chatter for tracking and monitoring.

Mail notifications to Sellers

  • You can enable/disable mail notifications to sellers.
  • If enabled, the seller receives an auto-mail whenever a new quote is submitted for his product on Odoo Marketplace.
  • Website View
  • Admin View
  • Seller View

Request Quote Button on the allowed products for wholesale buyers

The buyers can fill the quote form i.e. quantity needed and proposed price per unit

  • The buyer can view the sent quote and their status in his website account
  • Two way communicate channel for buyer and Odoo marketplace admin via Odoo Chatter in the quotation form
  • The buyer can add the approved quote directly to the cart from the quotation form

Enable mail notifications to seller when a new quote is submitted for their product

  • The admin can Enable/disable quote on all Marketplace Products
  • Quick Dashboard View for admin to track quotes from for all sellers’ products
  • The admin can see all quotes on all the products from the buyers
  • The admin can approve/reject the quote for any seller

Quick Dashboard View for Sellers to track quotes on their Marketplace products

  • The admin can Enable/disable quote on all Marketplace Products
  • The seller can see sale quotes from the buyers on his products only
  • The seller can approve/reject the quote from the quote form
